PROJECT SUMMARY

TableRush is a casual restaurant management game.

Player loop:

Seat customer
Take order
Cook food
Deliver food
Collect payment
Clean table
Repeat faster and faster

The game is NOT trying to be:

Realistic
Hardcore
Complex simulator

The game IS trying to be:

Fun
Fast
Easy to understand
Family friendly
Addictive
Mobile-quality

Think:

Cooking Fever
Diner Dash
Good Pizza Great Pizza

NOT:

Management dashboard
Tycoon spreadsheet
